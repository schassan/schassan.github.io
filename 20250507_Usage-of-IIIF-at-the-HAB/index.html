Metadata for Digital Facsimiles

  • captureType
    • singlePage, opening, detail
  • completeness
    • complete (different degrees possible: bookblock, cover, spine and edges)
    • partial (different degrees possible: coherent pages, example pages)
  • digitizationType
    • color, greyscale, bw, thermo, spectral, microscope, threeD, other
  • sourceType
    • original, reproduction
  • Controlled vocabulary at https://normdaten.staatsbibliothek-berlin.de/vocabulary/

Re-Publication of Digital Facsimiles

  • Need to re-publish all digital facsimile, because of the age of the presentation
    • delivery of static jpg-files
    • no zoom, no other image manipulation possible
  • Some digital facsimile had been published as IIIF in the course of the project Manuscripts from German-Speaking Lands – A Polonsky Foundation Digitization Project → test implementation
  • Re-structuring of image storage
    • grouping by object type (mss, prints, etc) and shelf mark
    • more than one disc lead to possible multiple digitisation
  • Re-grouping into subcollections according to metadata information
  • from → /master/mss01/mss/27-6-poet-1/00001.tif
    to → /images/mss/27-6-poet/2005_standard_original/27-6-poet_00001.jp2

Structuring the IIIF manifests

  • more than one sequence and/or range
    • grouped by metadata combination
  • more than one image per canvas
  • „reconstruction“ of volumes (re-arrange, virtual binding)
